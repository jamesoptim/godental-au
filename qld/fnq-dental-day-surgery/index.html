About FNQ Dental & Day Surgery

FNQ Dental & Day Surgery is a comprehensive dental practice and day surgery facility in Cairns, serving Far North Queensland. The practice offers a full range of dental services from general dentistry to complex surgical procedures including wisdom teeth removal under general anaesthesia. Their modern facilities and experienced team provide high-quality dental care in a tropical setting.
