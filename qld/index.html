Your Guide to Dentists in Queensland

Dental Care Across Queensland

With a warm climate and active lifestyle, Queenslanders place a premium on dental health and aesthetics. Brisbane and the Gold Coast are home to numerous cosmetic and general dental practices, while the Sunshine Coast and Toowoomba offer well-established clinics serving growing populations.

North Queensland cities like Townsville and Cairns have developed strong dental services in recent years, including specialist and surgical practices. If you're relocating to or visiting Queensland, the listings above are a great starting point for finding quality dental care.

Frequently Asked Questions About Dentists in Queensland

Browse the QLD listings above to find dental practices on the Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, and throughout South-East Queensland. Each listing includes a full address and phone number for easy contact.

Many Queensland dental practices offer Saturday morning appointments, and some larger practices in Brisbane and the Gold Coast are open on Sundays for emergencies. Check individual listings or call the practice to confirm weekend availability.

Queensland has a wide range of dental specialists including orthodontists, periodontists, oral surgeons, and prosthodontists. Many general practices also offer specialist services in-house, such as dental implants and Invisalign. Browse the services listed on each practice page for details.

Most Queensland dental practices accept all major health funds and offer on-the-spot HICAPS claiming. Some also offer gap-free preventive treatments for eligible fund members. Contact the practice directly for details about your specific fund.
