About NT Oral Health Services Darwin

NT Oral Health Services Darwin is the Northern Territory Government's public dental service, based at the Royal Darwin Hospital campus in Tiwi. The service provides dental care to eligible patients including children, concession card holders, and emergency patients. Their team of public dental professionals delivers essential dental care to the Top End community.
