About The Paddington Dental Surgery

The Paddington Dental Surgery is a well-established dental practice on Oxford Street, serving the Paddington and surrounding Eastern Suburbs community. Known for their gentle approach and personalised care, the practice offers a full range of dental services in a welcoming, heritage-listed building. The team combines traditional values of patient care with modern dental techniques.
