About The Denture Centre

The Denture Centre is a specialist denture clinic in Hobart, providing expert denture services to southern Tasmania. Located on Elizabeth Street in the Hobart CBD, the practice specialises in all types of dentures including full dentures, partial dentures, and implant-retained dentures. Their experienced dental prosthetists create custom-fitted dentures for optimal comfort and function.
