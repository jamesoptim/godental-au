About Franklin Bright Smiles

Franklin Bright Smiles is a modern dental practice in Canberra's growing Franklin suburb, providing quality dental care for the Gungahlin district. The practice offers a range of general, cosmetic, and preventive dental services with a friendly team committed to creating bright, healthy smiles.
